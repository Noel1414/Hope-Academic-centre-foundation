About Hope Academic Center Foundation

Hope Academic Centre Foundation (HACF) was officially registered in 2023, although its journey began in 2021 with the establishment of Hope Academic Centre, a school founded through a strategic partnership agreement. This partnership has since become a model that guides our mission to eradicate poverty through community empowerment initiatives. HACF is a charitable organization committed to ensuring that every child not only gains access to education but receives quality education. We aim to shift the mindset of private educational institutions towards a more socially responsible approachâ€”one that recognizes the future of any community is rooted in the capacity and potential of its individual members. In alignment with our long-term strategy, HACF is establishing four key operational arms to drive our mission forward. These organs, which function under the umbrella of Hope Academic Centre Foundation, are designed to address specific areas of community development:

Hope Academic Centre

Our foundational school and educational model.

Community Education Development Accord (CEDA)

Focused on grassroots educational development and policy advocacy..

Women for Community Development Network (WCDN)

Dedicated to empowering women through education, skills training, and leadership initiatives..

Learn and Lead Initiative (L&LI)

A youth-focused program promoting leadership, mentorship, and civic engagement..

Transparency & Accountability Enterprise Network (TAEN)

A governance body ensuring integrity, transparency, and accountability within our programs and partnerships.

Together, these organs form a holistic framework for empowering individuals and transforming communities through education, inclusion, and innovation.
